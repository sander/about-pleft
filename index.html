Privacy

Your privacy is very important to us. We will not share any personally identifiable data with third parties. We will also not look at it ourselves.

Just like you, we don’t like spam. Pleft will only send emails directly related to your appointments.

If you’ve got any further questions regarding to privacy, please contact us and we’ll reply quickly.

The founding of Pleft

Two students from Amsterdam saw something weird happening on the internet. It became really easy to meet your friends online. Easier than meeting up in real life.

They couldn’t let this happen. Even the Queen was worried. To save humanity from emoticonization, they decided to make real life contact easy.

And then Pleft was born.
